Where Lyriem Began

We built what we wish had existed all along.

Lyriem started with a group of freelancers and founders who'd all lived the same frustrations. Businesses focused on stretching their budgets. Freelancers working harder for less. And traditional freelance platforms taking a cut from both sides to pad their bottom line.

So we built a platform that succeeds when its users do — not one that skims from their success.
